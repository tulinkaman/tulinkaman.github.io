About

I am the Verena Meyer Visiting Professor in the Department of Informatics at the University of Zurich and an affiliated associate professor in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Arkansas.

Research

Tulin Kaman’s research focuses on understanding the dynamics of turbulence due to hydrodynamic instabilities and quantifying uncertainties in model and input parameters using high-performance computing systems efficiently. During her fellowship, she started performing convergence studies to assess the impact of space and temporal resolutions on numerical weather predictions, using the theory of sensitivity analysis.
